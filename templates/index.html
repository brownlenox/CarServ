01
WHATS A TURBO CHARGER ?
Turbocharger (turbo, turbine, turbo-compressor) - is a device that increases the total power of the internal combustion engine, using the kinetic energy of exhaust gases.
In fact, from a technically correct position, to call a turbocharger a turbine is technically wrong!
Because: Turbocharger is a turbo-compressor or turbocompressor. The turbine is its HOT part of turbocharger or turbo-compressor. A turbocharger has the compressor powered by a turbine.
02
SIGNS OF TURBOCHARGER MALFUCTION
(1) When turbocharger is noisy when working eg like a whisle sound
(2) There is oil on the joints of the air pipe
(3) Reduction of the accelaration of the car caused by lose of power by the car
03
CAUSES OF TURBOCHARGER MALFUNCTIONS
(1) Old contaminated engine oil and aggresive environment.
- Oil from the engine goes to the turbocharger Containing foreign particles it will deteriorating its moving parts, scratching and damaging its bushings and bearings.
(2) Overheating at extreme operating condition of the turbo
- malfunction of turbo control system, a faulty air pressure sensor leads to foreign formations on the shaft and wheel, which contributes to their overheating.
When overheated, violation of the temperature characteristics of the turbocharger leads to deformation of the turbine parts, the formation of cracks on the nozzles of the shaft.
(3) Damage turbo by foreign
-Having entered into the turbocharger, solid foreign objects will inflict irreparable damage to its parts. As a result of inaccurate repair or installation, an external washer, nut, plastic, trapped in the turbocharger, will damage the turbocharger wheel or the entire turbocharger shaft
